WeCanChangeOurWorld aims to be a change agent for brands serious about sustainable transformation in South Africa – The portal is a platform for all companies and organisations involved in sustainable brand programmes and initiatives to connect with influencers and non-governmental/governmental organisations involved in sustainable social transformation in South Africa.
WeCanChangeOurWorld is an information destination for all news, expert commentary, regulatory data, case studies and general records for those committed to investing in true sustainable initiatives – This highly dynamic industry sector has many passionate activists involved in worthy causes; NGOs driving social projects and companies as well as organisations needing to implement CHANGE programmes benefiting from meaningful changes within their organisational structures. In order to drive real, meaningful social change, it is critical to put brands in touch with measurable social entrepreneurship , something WeCanChangeOurWorld is committed to doing. Our expert Editorial Board will guide editorial policy to ensure we deliver credible, independent information and insight.
WeCanChangeOurWorld enables companies, organisations, NGOs and all services involved in the sustainable social transformation space to promote their initiatives, projects and programmes; highlight successful case studies and disseminate their news to the broader community of people passionate about and interested in social change. This independent news and information web portal for social development will aim to enable brands to collaborate with the NGO sector ; debunk the myths of a non-cooperative sector which operates in silos ; provide information on the latest regulatory requirements and the triple bottom line ; as well as measurement models with correct ROI (return on investment) as opposed to once-off charitable investment. An expert Advisory Board will guide our Management Team to ensure we remain independent and credible agents for social change in South Africa and Africa and for this patform to remain neutral and report on developments for all agendas.
